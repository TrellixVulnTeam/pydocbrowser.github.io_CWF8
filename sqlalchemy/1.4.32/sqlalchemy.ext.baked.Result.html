class Result(object): (source)

View In Hierarchy

Invokes a :class:`.BakedQuery` against a :class:`.Session`.

The :class:`_baked.Result` object is where the actual :class:`.query.Query` object gets created, or retrieved from the cache, against a target :class:`.Session`, and is then invoked for results.
