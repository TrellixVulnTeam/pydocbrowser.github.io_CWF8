The reverse of :func:`parse_locale`. It creates a locale identifier out of a ``(language, territory, script, variant)`` tuple. Items can be set to ``None`` and trailing ``None``\s can also be left out of the tuple.

>>> get_locale_identifier(('de', 'DE', None, '1999'))
'de_DE_1999'

.. versionadded:: 1.0

:param tup: the tuple as returned by :func:`parse_locale`. :param sep: the separator for the identifier.
