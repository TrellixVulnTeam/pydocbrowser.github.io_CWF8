def _fixerTest(self, sentenceData, expected=None, exceptionClass=None): (source)

A generic adapter fixer test.

Creates a sentence from the C{sentenceData} and sends that to the adapter. If C{exceptionClass} is not passed, this is assumed to work, and C{expected} is compared with the adapter's internal state. Otherwise, passing the sentence to the adapter is checked to raise C{exceptionClass}.

@param sentenceData: Raw sentence content. @type sentenceData: C{dict} mapping C{str} to C{str}

@param expected: The expected state of the adapter. @type expected: C{dict} or L{None}

@param exceptionClass: The exception to be raised by the adapter. @type exceptionClass: subclass of C{Exception}
