class ServiceManager(service.MultiService): (source)

View In Hierarchy

Business logic for a managed component connection to a Jabber router.

This service maintains a single connection to a Jabber router and provides facilities for packet routing and transmission. Business logic modules are services implementing L{ijabber.IService} (like subclasses of L{Service}), and added as sub-service.
