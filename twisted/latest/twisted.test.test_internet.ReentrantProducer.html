Similar to L{DummyProducer}, but with a resumeProducing method which calls back into an L{IConsumer} method of the consumer against which it is registered.

@ivar consumer: The consumer with which this producer has been or will be registered.

@ivar methodName: The name of the method to call on the consumer inside C{resumeProducing}.

@ivar methodArgs: The arguments to pass to the consumer method invoked in C{resumeProducing}.
