class ShapedProtocolFactory: (source)

View In Hierarchy

Dispense C{Protocols} with traffic shaping on their transports.

Usage:

myserver = SomeFactory()
myserver.protocol = ShapedProtocolFactory(myserver.protocol,
                                          bucketFilter)

Where C{SomeServerFactory} is a L{twisted.internet.protocol.Factory}, and C{bucketFilter} is an instance of L{HierarchicalBucketFilter}.
