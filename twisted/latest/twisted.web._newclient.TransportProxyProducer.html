An L{twisted.internet.interfaces.IPushProducer} implementation which wraps another such thing and proxies calls to it until it is told to stop.

@ivar _producer: The wrapped L{twisted.internet.interfaces.IPushProducer} provider or L{None} after this proxy has been stopped.
