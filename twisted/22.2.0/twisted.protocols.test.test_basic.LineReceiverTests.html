def test_notQuiteMaximumLineLengthUnfinished(self): (source)
LineReceiver doesn't disconnect the transport it if receives a non-finished line whose length, counting the delimiter, is longer than its MAX_LENGTH but shorter than its MAX_LENGTH + len(delimiter). (When the first part that exceeds the max is the beginning of the delimiter.)
