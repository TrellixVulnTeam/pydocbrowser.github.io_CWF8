class _CachedEpydocLinker(_EpydocLinker): (source)

Known subclasses: pydoctor.test.test_epydoc2stan._TestCachedEpydocLinker

View In Hierarchy

This linker implements smart caching functionalities on top of public methods defined in DocstringLinker.

The cache is implemented at the Tag (Stan) level, letting us do transformation over cached Tag instances and recycle already resolved URLs and adjust them to change formatting as requested by link_xref().
