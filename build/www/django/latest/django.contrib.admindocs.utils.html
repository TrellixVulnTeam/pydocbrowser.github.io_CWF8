Find named groups in `pattern` and replace them with the group name. E.g.,

  1. ^(?P<a>\w+)/b/(\w+)$ ==> ^<a>/b/(\w+)$
  2. ^(?P<a>\w+)/b/(?P<c>\w+)/$ ==> ^<a>/b/<c>/$
  3. ^(?P<a>\w+)/b/(\w+) ==> ^<a>/b/(\w+)

Find unnamed groups in `pattern` and replace them with '<var>'. E.g.,

  1. ^(?P<a>\w+)/b/(\w+)$ ==> ^(?P<a>\w+)/b/<var>$
