Construct a L{_ContextFactoryToConnectionFactory} with a L{twisted.internet.interfaces.IOpenSSLContextFactory}.

Immediately call C{getContext} on C{oldStyleContextFactory} in order to force advance parameter checking, since old-style context factories don't actually check that their arguments to L{OpenSSL} are correct.
