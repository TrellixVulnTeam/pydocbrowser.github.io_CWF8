def _maybeCleanupScopeIndex(family, packed): (source)

On FreeBSD, kill the embedded interface indices in link-local scoped addresses.

@param family: The address family of the packed address - one of the
I{socket.AF_*} constants.
@param packed: The packed representation of the address (ie, the bytes of a
I{in_addr} field).

@type packed: L{bytes}

@return: The packed address with any FreeBSD-specific extra bits cleared. @rtype: L{bytes}

@see: U{https://twistedmatrix.com/trac/ticket/6843} @see: U{http://www.freebsd.org/doc/en/books/developers-handbook/ipv6.html#ipv6-scope-index}

@note: Indications are that the need for this will be gone in FreeBSD >=10.
