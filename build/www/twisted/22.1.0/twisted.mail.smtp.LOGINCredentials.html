L{LOGINCredentials} generates challenges for I{LOGIN} authentication.

For interoperability with Outlook, the challenge generated does not exactly match the one defined in the U{draft specification<http://sepp.oetiker.ch/sasl-2.1.19-ds/draft-murchison-sasl-login-00.txt>}.
